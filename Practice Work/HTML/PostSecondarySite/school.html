Post-Secondary Opportunities

1.) Seneca College (Computer Programming Program)

This two-year diploma program prepares students with skills and knowledge to begin their career as a programmer. Seneca offers the largest computer studies program in the province. Through a variety of practical and theoretical courses, students will gain skills in mobile development, web development and distributed programming techniques and database administration. Students will also learn about security and a variety of operating systems including Windows, UNIX, Linux, and IMB Business Computing.

Durationn: 2 Years

Cost: $3858/Year

Requirements:

  • Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD)
  • Grade 12 English (ENG4C or ENG4U)
  • One of the following courses: Grade 11 Functions and Applications (MCF3M), Grade 11 Functions (MCR3U), Grade 12 Foundations for College Mathematics (MAP4C), Grade 12 Mathematics for College Technology (MCT4C), Grade 12 Mathematics of Data Management (MDM4U), Grade 12 Advanced Functions (MHF4U) -or- Grade 12 Calculus and Vectors (MCV4U)

2.) Sheridan College (Computer Programmer Program)

Sheridan's Computer Programmer diploma offers highly marketable skills in the field of information technology. A practical applied approach is taken with an emphasis on individual programming skills including the mobile environment, communication skills, problem solving and teamwork. The applied approach is facilitated by an interactive classroom experience for each student, their own personal laptop and the most current versions of industry standard computer programming software available through Sheridan.

Durationn: 2 Years

Cost: $4765/Year

Requirements:

  • Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD)
  • Grade 12 English (ENG4C or ENG4U)
  • One of the following courses: Grade 11 Functions and Applications (MCF3M), Grade 11 Functions (MCR3U), Grade 12 Foundations for College Mathematics (MAP4C), Grade 12 Mathematics for College Technology (MCT4C), Grade 12 Mathematics of Data Management (MDM4U), Grade 12 Advanced Functions (MHF4U) -or- Grade 12 Calculus and Vectors (MCV4U)

3.) Algonquin College (Computer Programmer - Co-Op Program / Perth Campus)

The program specializes in program development strategies (using object oriented modelling), database design, and database administration. Students will use leading industry software products such as Oracle and CASE tools. They will also learn about programming languages such as Java, COBOL, SQL and PHP. Students will study object-oriented analysis and design, operating systems and coding in integrated environments and how to debug, test, and maintain codes.

Durationn: 2 Years

Cost: $6061.62/Year (Co-Op) / $3720.82/Year (Perth Campus)

Requirements:

  • Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD)
  • Grade 12 English (ENG4C or ENG4U)
  • Grade 12 college-level or higher Math (MAP4C, MCT4C, MDM4U, MHF4U or MCV4U)

4.) Carleton University (Bachelor of Computer Science Major)

Students will learn the principles that will help them adapt to changing technology. They will study the theory, design, and implementation of computer applications and systems. They will learn to use computing and information technology to help solve problems faced in business, science, and society today and tomorrow.

Durationn: 4 Years

Cost: $11 257.01/Year

Requirements:

  • Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD)
  • Grade 12 Advanced Functions (MHF4U) -or- Grade 12 Calculus and Vectors (MCV4U)
  • Five additional Grade 12 university-level or university/college-level courses
